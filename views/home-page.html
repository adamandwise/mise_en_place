About Mise En Place

Mise En Place is a revolutionary web application designed specifically for the restaurant industry. It offers a comprehensive solution for managing and accessing recipes, both for preparation and service, directly from a desktop web browser, or from a mobile device. This innovative app streamlines the recipe management process, providing restaurateurs with an effortless way to update, edit, and access their entire recipe database with ease.

In addition to its convenience, Mise En Place also offers a valuable training tool for new hires. With access to all the recipes they'll need to know, they can practice and familiarize themselves with the menu outside the kitchen, ensuring a smooth transition into their new role.

With Mise En Place, restaurants can streamline their operations, improve efficiency, and provide a more enjoyable and safer dining experience for their guests. Upgrade your kitchen operations today with the power of Mise En Place.

Meet The Team

Adam, Tom, and Sukanvisa are a dynamic team of software development students at Green River College in Auburn, Washington. With a diverse range of backgrounds and expertise, they have come together to create Mise En Place, a cutting-edge web application designed specifically for the restaurant industry.

Adam, a seasoned chef with a decade of experience in the culinary arts, brings his deep understanding of the service industry to the project, ensuring that Mise En Place is both useful and intuitive for its users.

Tom, an accomplished motorcycle mechanic, brings a unique attention to detail honed from working on complex combustion systems, ensuring that the app is meticulously crafted and reliable.

Sukanvisa, who holds a bachelors degree in psychology, brings a crucial understanding of the human mind to the project. Her insights are critical to the success of building and marketing a user-friendly product that truly meets the needs of real people.

With their collective expertise, Adam, Tom, and Sukanvisa are poised to revolutionize the way restaurants manage their recipes and operations. Trust in their expertise and join the Mise En Place revolution today.
