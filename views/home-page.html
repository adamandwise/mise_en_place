About Mise En Place

Mise En Place is a revolutionary web application designed specifically for the restaurant industry. It offers a comprehensive solution for managing and accessing recipes, both for preparation and service, directly from a web browser on their mobile device. This innovative app streamlines the recipe management process, providing restaurateurs with an effortless way to update, edit, and access their entire recipe database with ease.

In addition to its convenience, Mise En Place also offers a valuable training tool for new hires. With access to all the recipes they'll need to know, they can practice and familiarize themselves with the menu outside the kitchen, ensuring a smooth transition into their new role.

With Mise En Place, restaurants can streamline their operations, improve efficiency, and provide a more enjoyable and safer dining experience for their guests. Upgrade your kitchen operations today with the power of Mise En Place.

Meet The Team

We are the team of three with the hope of creating a user-friendly website targeting restaurant industry. We met by chance in class and with great work ethics, communication skills and individual life skills, Mise En Place was born.
